# Phase 16 — Procedural Everything

**Goal:** Every aspect of the game world can be procedurally generated — terrain, buildings, roads, citizens, history, culture. No hand-authored content required.

## 16.1 Procedural History
- Generate a city's backstory: founding year, major events, architectural eras
- History affects building styles, monument placement, district names
- "This city was founded as a mining town in 1850, grew through industrialisation..."

## 16.2 Procedural Culture
- Regional building styles (Victorian London, Haussmann Paris, Colonial New England)
- Style mixing at district boundaries
- Cultural landmarks generated from history

## 16.3 Procedural Terrain (Advanced)
- Erosion simulation: water carves valleys over geological time
- Tectonic features: mountain ranges, fault lines
- Biome generation: forest, grassland, desert, tundra based on climate
- Underground geology: rock layers, aquifers, mineral deposits

## 16.4 Procedural Citizens
- Generated names, appearances, personalities
- Social networks: families, friends, coworkers
- Behavioural archetypes affecting daily routines
- Life events: marriages, births, education, career changes

## 16.5 Procedural Music
- Adaptive soundtrack generated from city state
- Era-appropriate instrumentation
- Mood matching: calm for parks, tense for disasters, triumphant for milestones

## Exit Criteria
- [ ] Generate a complete city with 10,000+ year history from a single seed
- [ ] Each citizen has a unique name and personality
- [ ] Terrain shows erosion patterns
- [ ] Music adapts to gameplay mood in real-time
