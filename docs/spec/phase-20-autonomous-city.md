# Phase 20 — Autonomous City & Agent Intelligence

**Goal:** Citizens become truly intelligent agents with emergent behaviour. The city runs itself — the player becomes an observer and guide, not a micromanager.

## 20.1 Belief-Desire-Intention (BDI) Agent Architecture
- Each citizen has beliefs (what they know about the world)
- Desires (goals: get a better job, find housing, start a family)
- Intentions (current plan to achieve a desire)
- Plans are generated dynamically, not scripted

## 20.2 Social Network Simulation
- Citizens form relationships: family, friends, coworkers
- Influence propagation: happy citizens make neighbours happier
- Social events: parties, protests, celebrations
- Opinion dynamics: citizens discuss and change views on policies

## 20.3 Emergent Behaviour
- Traffic patterns emerge from individual decisions (no global controller)
- Neighbourhood character emerges from resident demographics
- Business clusters form organically (restaurants near offices)
- Gentrification: improving districts attract wealthier citizens

## 20.4 City Council AI
- AI councillors propose policies based on citizen sentiment
- Public hearings: citizens voice opinions
- Elections: player can be voted out if satisfaction drops
- Opposition: citizen groups form to protest unpopular policies

## 20.5 Historical Memory
- Citizens remember events (the flood of year 5, the boom of year 12)
- City develops a cultural identity over generations
- Monuments and plaques placed automatically for significant events
- Oral history: citizens tell stories about the city's past

## Exit Criteria
- [ ] Citizens make autonomous housing and job decisions
- [ ] Social networks affect satisfaction and migration
- [ ] Business clusters form without player intervention
- [ ] AI council proposes and enacts policies
- [ ] Citizens reference historical events in their behaviour
